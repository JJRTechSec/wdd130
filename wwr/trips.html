What We Offer

Rafting and Accommodation

6 hours rafting and 2 nights

This trip offers guests 6 hours of rafting (2hrs rafting each day across 3 days). This allows groups to sufficiently enjoy the rapids on offer, while also being free to go off and explore as they wish.
Also included in this offering is lunch(x2) and dinner(x2) for each guest. Each guest room has a mini-fridge to store drinks/breakfast items (not provided).

Bachelor/Bachelorette Parties

3 hours rafting, spa access, unforgettable memories

Our bachelor/bachelorette party package offers guests un unforgettable experience, with 3 hours of rafting, on the course of your choice. Access to our spa area*, and an all-you-can-eat buffet to end the experience. This package includes a set of pictures and videos taken all along the course, ensuring a trip to remember.

* Access to hot-tubs, saunas and ice baths - massages are not included but can be added during booking.

Company/School Trips

3 hours rafting, meeting rooms, lunch or dinner

This package is tailor-made for companies or schools wishing to offer team-building experiences, or just a fun day out.
3 hours of rafting for all guests, access to our state-of-the-art meeting rooms, and a choice of either lunch or dinner for every guest.
